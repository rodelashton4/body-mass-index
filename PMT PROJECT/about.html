About BMI Tracker

BMI Tracker was created to help individuals understand and monitor their Body Mass Index (BMI) in a simple, accessible way. Our mission is to provide accurate BMI calculations along with personalized health recommendations to help you maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, BMI Tracker started as a university project aimed at making health metrics more understandable for the general public. Since then, we've grown into a comprehensive platform offering not just BMI calculations, but also exercise recommendations, nutrition advice, and health tracking tools.
